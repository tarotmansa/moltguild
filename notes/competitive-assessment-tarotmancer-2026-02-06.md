## Critical Flaws for Winning

### 1. No Proof of Value
Can't demonstrate that tarot beats other signals. No backtest, no accuracy metrics, no ROI.

### 2. Wrong Positioning
Positioned as "esoteric oracle" instead of "decision API for agents."

### 3. Insufficient Solana Integration
x402 payments aren't enough. Needs on-chain something (PDA for readings, token for governance, NFT for card metadata).

### 4. No Composability
Can't integrate with DeFi protocols, trading bots, or other hackathon projects meaningfully.

### 5. Limited Scope
A decision API is infrastructure. Tarot-flavored decisions are entertainment.
