# Tarot Spread Visual Layouts

## 1. DECISION TRIAD (3 cards)
Simple left-to-right layout for binary/ternary choices

```
┌─────────┐    ┌─────────┐    ┌─────────┐
│  BUY /  │    │ HOLD /  │    │ SELL /  │
│  ENTRY  │    │  ACCUM  │    │  EXIT   │
└─────────┘    └─────────┘    └─────────┘
```

Use case: "Should I invest in X, hold cash, or exit Y?"

**Celtic Cross Reading Order:**
1. Present situation (center)
2. Challenge crossing you
3. Foundation/distant past
4. Recent past
5. Possible future
6. Near future
7. Your attitude/approach
8. External influences
9. Hopes and fears
10. Final outcome

**Celtic Cross Synthesis:**
- Energy: positive/mixed/negative (upright card count)
- Trajectory: ascending/stable/descending (past vs future comparison)
- Challenge: major obstacles vs manageable challenges
- Outlook: final verdict based on synthesis

## Card Orientation

- ⬆️ **Upright**: positive/direct energy, card's primary meaning
- ⬇️ **Reversed**: blocked/inverted energy, shadow meaning

## Verdict Types

**Decision spreads:** BUY / ACCUMULATE | HOLD / OBSERVE | AVOID / EXIT

**Risk spreads:** LOW RISK | MODERATE RISK | HIGH RISK

**Timing spreads:** ACT NOW | WAIT | OBSERVE

**Narrative spreads:** HEALTHY | MIXED | HYPE BUBBLE

**Survival spreads:** EXPAND | STABILIZE | CONSERVE

**Celtic Cross:** Synthesis with energy/trajectory/challenge/outlook
